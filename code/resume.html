Hello, I'm Lilin.

A Straight-A student, a Problem-solver, a Dedicated and Enthusiastic Learner.

I pursued a degree in Public Affairs Management in China and landed a job as a clerk in a state-owned enterprise after graduation. Although I spent almost a year working in a traditional company, I came to the realization that I wanted to expand my horizons and seek out new challenges. That's when I decided to pursue my passion for programming and enrolled in Algonquin College in 2022.

After enrolling in college, I gradually felt the charm of computer science. Although assignments sometimes took a lot of time, the moment I successfully completed them gave a great sense of achievement. Writing code has become my passion, where I can solve technical and logical problems, both creatively and efficiently.

I enjoy discussing challenging issues with my classmates and working together to solve them. We can all learn a lot from each other, and I look forward to learning more about the technology field. In college, we mainly studied Java programming language, databases, operating systems, web development, etc.

I am determined to maintain my excellent academic performance and secure a coveted coop position. My ultimate goal is to become a skilled programmer and realize my passion for technology. With a focus on constant learning, I am eager to embark on this journey and unlock new opportunities to fulfill my dream of becoming a successful software engineer.
